 About Me
My name is Klaus, and I'm a student at Ritsumeikan University, diving deep into the world of web development. When I first started learning HTML and CSS, I was overwhelmed by all the tags and properties. But over time, I’ve come to enjoy how these elements fit together like digital LEGO bricks. I’m passionate about turning ideas into interactive websites. Outside of coding, I enjoy hiking in Japan’s beautiful countryside, exploring cozy cafés, and playing video games with friends. I also love dogs and often take long walks with my husky back home. This website is a part of my journey into becoming a better developer. Every section here reflects something I’ve learned this semester. From creating layouts using tables to adding interactivity with JavaScript—it’s all been a valuable experience. I hope to use these skills in the future for real-world projects and even freelance work. Thanks for visiting my site. I hope you find it fun and inspiring!
